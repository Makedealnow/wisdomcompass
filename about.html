About Wisdom

Wisdom for Life Decisions.

Wisdom helps people navigate life decisions by connecting real-world situations with biblical principles, scripture, and timeless wisdom.

What Wisdom does

Users describe a situation and receive guidance based on scripture, biblical stories, and biblical principles.

What Wisdom does not do

Wisdom is not a full Bible app, church app, devotional app, prayer app, legal advisor, medical advisor, financial advisor, therapist, or emergency service.

Our mission

To help people slow down, reflect, and make better life decisions through biblical wisdom.
